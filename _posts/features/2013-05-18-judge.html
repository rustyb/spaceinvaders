Be Judged. Got to win!

Because what's a competition without being able to to win something? Your ideas are what is most important, and it is up to you to decide what presentation format works best for you. The projects will be evaluated against the following best practice criteria contained in the Urban Design Manual:

Judging Criteria. Aim High!

The judging criteria are based on principals from the urban design manual. Below is the list of criteria for judging extracted from the manual which will be used by the judging panel.

  1. Context
  2. Connections
  3. Inclusivity
  4. Variety
  5. Efficiency
  6. Distinctiveness
  7. Layout
  8. Public Realm
