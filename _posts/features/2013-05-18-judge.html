Defend your Idea. Be Judged!

Regardless of whether you are from north, east, west or south, you've got the skills, you have the talent! Get involved, put your ideas to the test. This is your time to shine!

Here's something to aim for. Ready, Steady, GO!

Your project will be assessed against a selection of principles from the Urban Design Manual as outlined below:

  1. Context
  2. Connections
  3. Inclusivity
  4. Variety
  5. Efficiency
  6. Distinctiveness
  7. Layout
  8. Public Realm
